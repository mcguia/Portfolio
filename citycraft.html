CityCraft

We broke down the problems expressed by the Eddy VNA employees from our first focus group meeting. The lack of communication was astounding and was repeated many times by members of the Eddy VNA group.
This is an early mock-up of the interface we designed for the Eddy VNA teams. It would list all team members and all patients. It would also give notifications for each patient update for the employees responsible for them. It's not unlike Facebook so the employees who use it will adapt easily, yet it does not have any superfluous extras like the social network as this is for a work environment.

CityCraft is a game designed to bring young urban students, living in low income areas, into STEM education, and hopefully on the path to a STEM career. A prominent point in our research was that current STEM education is often exclusive to those who have the funds to pay for the extra education or materials, so we designed something all inclusive for students. It combines role-playing and active learning through experience to introduce the students to jobs such as civil engineering and urban planning. See the manual here!
