The focus of this project was not to design something according to a prompt, but rather to physically dissect a piece of technology and examine how it is used. An Xbox controller does not just control a video game character, it controls the whole social network: Xbox Live and its separate components. The device dictates who can use it and who cannot. Basic technology has a lot more impact than just being parts in a system. Through the Xbox controller, we analyzed the social and cultural impact along with user experience and demographics to get a larger picture. As designers, we have to take these things into consideration for what we plan to create and invent in the future.
